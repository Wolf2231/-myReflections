   The staging area is the place where files that is changed are commited.

change?

   If multiple logical changes made it is farewell to add one file to staging area

How do the diagrams help you visualize the branch structure?

   It gives us graphical visuals of where branches are connected to the master branch
   and identify what to expect to find in the branch we created from the master branch.

the diagram the way we do?

   It results a combined line, organized be the recently added. It is presented in this
   diagram this way, to insure a consistance flow.
